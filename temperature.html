Temperature Information

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as location approaches the equator. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere (latitude < 0), at the time data was collected, tends to be warmer relative to the northern counterpart. This may be due to the tilt of the earth.
